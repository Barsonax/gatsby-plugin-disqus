---
title: "Motivation"
---

The goal of this plugin is to allow users to bring their content to life and cultivate engaged communities by integrating  [Disqus](https://disqus.com/) comments into their blazing-fast [Gatsby](https://www.gatsbyjs.org/) websites. After struggling to integrate a few existing React components into my Gatsby site, creating an easily-configurable plugin for the Gatsby ecosystem felt like a no-brainer.
